loaded chilli cheese fries


ingredients

  • potatoes
  • paprika
  • red chili powder
  • black pepper
  • salt
  • cumin seeds
  • garlic powder
  • cornflour
  • flour
  • oil
  • milk
  • cheese
  • red leicester
  • jalepenos
  • peri peri seasoning
  • jalepenos

Instructions

  1. start off with 4 medium sized potatoes
  2. cut into thick pieces
  3. soak in cold water for 15 mins
  4. 2 tsp paprika
  5. 1/2 tsp black pepper
  6. 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  7. 1 tsp salt
  8. 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  9. 1 tsp garlic powder
  10. add 30g of cornflour
  11. 30g of plain flour
  12. mix everything up
  13. add 2-3 tbsp of water
  14. add the fries to a frying pan with oil on low heat
  15. fry for 2mins then gently stir as they`re frying
  16. fry for 7-8 mins in total
  17. start with your cheese sauce
  18. 50 ml of whole milk
  19. grate some cheese and add it too a pot with the milk
  20. cook this until the cheese melts and combine to make a sauce
  21. add 50g of red leicester
  22. add a little black pepper
  23. dont forget to add jalepenos with some peri peri seasoning
